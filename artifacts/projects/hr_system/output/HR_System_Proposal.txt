Project Name: HR System
Requirement Domain: HR Management System

System Overview:
This proposal outlines a comprehensive Cloud-based HR Management System designed to meet the needs of HR Managers, Employees, Recruiters, and Payroll Specialists. The system will provide a secure, scalable, and integrated platform for managing all core HR functions.

Core Features:
1. Employee Data Management: Centralized database for employee records.
2. Attendance and Leave Tracking: Integration with attendance tracking systems for real-time monitoring.
3. Payroll Processing: Seamless integration with payroll systems to automate salary calculations and disbursements.
4. Recruitment and Onboarding: Tools to manage job postings, applications, and new hire onboarding.
5. Performance Evaluation: Modules for setting goals, conducting reviews, and tracking performance.
6. Training and Development: Management of employee training programs and certifications.
7. Compliance and Reporting: Ensuring data privacy compliance and generating standard HR reports along with customizable analytics dashboards.

User Roles and Security:
- HR Manager, Employee, Recruiter, Payroll Specialist roles with role-based access control.
- Secure authentication mechanisms to protect sensitive data.
- Compliance with data privacy regulations.

Integration Requirements:
- Integration with existing payroll systems.
- Integration with attendance tracking systems.

Deployment:
- Cloud-based deployment for scalability, accessibility, and ease of maintenance.

Assumptions:
- Multi-role user access supported.
- Cloud deployment assumed for scalability and accessibility.

Recommended Scenario:
- Complete HR management system with all core features.
- Integrated with payroll and attendance systems.
- Secure with role-based access and data privacy compliance.
- Cloud-hosted with reporting and analytics capabilities.
